DISCOVER AND EXPLORE THE BEAUTY OF SAN CARLOS CITY,   PANGASINAN

VISION:

San Carlos City is envisioned to be the environment-friendly Agro-Industrial, Tourism and Education Center of Pangasinan with upbeat commercial activities structured on efficient services, harmonious relationships and peace towards sustainable development and improved qualify of life of its people.

MISSION:

To make San Carlos City the economic tiger of the North through highly disciplined and committed work force and improved agro-industrial, commercial, educational and tourism initiatives thus enhancing the quality of life of its people.
